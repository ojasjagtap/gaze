How to Use

  1. Click the extension icon or Start button
  2. Complete the calibration by looking at each point
  3. Read naturally - the page scrolls based on your gaze
  4. Use the floating control to pause or adjust settings

Tips

  • Sit at a comfortable distance from the screen
  • Ensure good lighting on your face
  • Minimize head movement for best results
  • Recalibrate if scrolling feels inaccurate
